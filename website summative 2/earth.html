EARTH

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known planet that supports life. It has a rich atmosphere, liquid water, and a stable climate that allows living organisms to survive. Earth is often called the “Blue Planet” because most of its surface is covered by oceans.

Quick Facts

  • About 71% of Earth's surface is covered by water
  • Earth is the only known planet with life
  • It takes 24 hours to complete one rotation
