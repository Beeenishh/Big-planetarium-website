MARS

Mars is known as the Red Planet due to its reddish appearance caused by iron oxide on its surface. It is the fourth planet from the Sun and has been a major focus of space exploration.

Quick Facts

  • Distance from Sun: 228 million km
  • Day length: 24.6 hours
  • Moons: 2 (Phobos and Deimos)
