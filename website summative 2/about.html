About Big Planetarium

Big Planetarium is an educational space website created to help users explore the Milky Way in a fun, simple, and accessible way. The website introduces visitors to planets, space facts, and the beauty of our galaxy through clear information, images, and interactive features.

The aim of Big Planetarium is to make space learning feel exciting instead of difficult. Whether the visitor is a student, a family member, or simply curious about astronomy, the website is designed to be easy to understand and enjoyable to use.
