VENUS

Venus is the second planet from the Sun and is often called Earth's sister planet because of its similar size. It has a very thick atmosphere that traps heat, making it the hottest planet in the solar system. Venus appears very bright in the sky and has been widely studied by scientists.

Quick Facts

  • Venus is the hottest planet in the solar system
  • Venus spins backwards compared to most planets
  • It is the brightest planet visible from Earth
